Description:
A 65-year-old woman with a recent history of acute necrotizing pancreatitis presented with a 5-day history of abdominal pain and fevers. Physical examination was notable for tenderness in the upper abdomen. Computed tomography of the abdomen is shown. What is the diagnosis?

Options:
- Cystic pancreatic neoplasm
- Intra-abdominal abscesses
- Pancreatic pseudocyst
- Solid pancreatic tumor
- Walled-off pancreatic necrosis

Answer:
Walled-off pancreatic necrosis
(success rate: 55%)
